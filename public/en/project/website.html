My portfolio website

This summer of 2023, I decided to put my web programming skills to the test by creating this website.

I took the initiative of creating this tool with the aim of putting myself forward, thanks to my computer achievements. This site is also the most effective way I've found to share my professional profile with you, with documents such as my CV.

In the context of my IT training course, this project is also a good way of reusing the knowledge I acquired during my first year.

Another reason why I wanted to do this project was my university portfolio.

As part of the BUT program, we have to create a portfolio on a university platform (in this case, Mahara). The problem is that the portfolio is deleted from the university's servers once we've completed our training (with no possibility of exporting it...).

So I took the step of creating a more durable platform that could follow me throughout my career.

To realize this project, I thought about offering you the best possible experience with a user-friendly interface.

To achieve this goal, I made numerous sketches to come up with a design that suited me. This project allowed me to showcase my creativity and imagination. It also made me aware of the progress I still have to make in terms of web development.

Of course, this project is far from complete: in addition to the various improvements to the site, I still have a lot of achievement to share with you. At present, this site is mostly written in HTML and CSS directly, with a few PHP programs. In particular, I'd like to make more use of JavaScript.
